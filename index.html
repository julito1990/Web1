Steve Jobs

This is Steve Jobs Life

  • Steve Jobs was born in San Francisco, 1955.
  • Steven was adopted by Paul and Clara Jobs.
  • Jobs attended a local school in California and later enrolled at Reed College.
  • In 1974, Jobs travelled with Daniel Kottke to India in search of spiritual enlightenment.
  • In 1976, Wozniak and Steve Jobs invented the first Apple I computer.
  • In 1984, Jobs designed the first Macintosh.
  • In 1985, removed from his managerial duties, Jobs resigned and left Apple.
  • In 1990, Steve Jobs found Next.
  • In 1996, Apple bought Next.
  • In 2000's, Steve Jobs return to apple.
  • In October 5th 2011 Steve Jobs died.
